Another Roadsie Attraction

Another Roadside Attraction is an ego-test. Can you admit you don't understand every metaphysical reference between the holiness of portabello mushrooms and a virgin's first date? Then maybe you'd enjoy this book. It's like a lazy lounge down a long river - you just have to go with the flow. And when you relax and go with the flow, sometimes you encounter really powerful ideas that make you challenge the fabric of society.
